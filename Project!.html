Best cars of the last 100 years


The first car also known as the "Motorwagen," was invented at 1886 by Karl Benz a german inventor, Benz also patented his own throttle system, spark plugs, gear shifters, a water radiator, a carburetor and other fundamentals to the automobile. Benz eventually built a car company that still exists today as the Daimler Group. Automobiles made an massive impact at our lives,since 1886 our lives became less difficult and it was easy to travel from one destination to another.
